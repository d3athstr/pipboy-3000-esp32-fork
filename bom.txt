# Upstream BOM:
5x Wago terminal blocks
1x Programmable WiFi board (ESP32 Dev Module; prefer 8/16MB flash)
1x Speaker
1x MP3 player (DFPlayer Mini + FAT32 micro SD card)
1x Micro SD card
40x Multicolor solder wire
100x 100ohm resistors
1x Temperature and humidity sensor (SHT31, I2C 0x44)
20x Orange LED 8mm
50x Warm white LED 5mm
1x 4.0" TFT SPI 480x320 v2.1 (LCDwiki-style, XPT2046 touch + microSD onboard).
   Controller unconfirmed (ILI9486 read off chip; may be ST7796/ILI9488).
   Driven via LovyanGFX, NOT TFT_eSPI. *** DISPLAY NOT YET WORKING (solid grey)
   — new screen on order; see DISPLAY-BRINGUP-LOG.md. Watch the LCD vs touch
   (t_*) vs SD (sd_*) pins — wiring to the wrong group = permanent grey. ***
1x 5-position rotary encoder

# Empire12 additions — upstream BOM has no power source:
1x 3.7V LiPo battery (with JST; keep BAT leads <3in). Size to run time / fit.
1x Adafruit PowerBoost 500C (#1944; TPS61090) — LiPo CHARGER + 5V boost in ONE.
   Don has this on hand. Replaces the earlier TP4056 + separate-boost plan
   (no TP4056 needed). 500mA cont / ~1A peak out; charges over its own microUSB.
1x SPDT slide switch — wires to the PowerBoost EN pin (EN<->GND off / EN<->BAT
   on). Carries no load current. See WIRING.md "Stage 2".
1x 1k resistor (ESP32 TX -> DFPlayer RX, kills idle hiss)
1x 100-470uF electrolytic cap (across DFPlayer 5V rail, prevents brownouts)

# v2 firmware additions (2026-07-08) — ESP32-S3 build:
1x ESP32-S3 DevKitC N16R8 (16MB flash/8MB PSRAM) — replaces classic ESP32
   *** ESP32 REPLACED 2026-07-12 (old board suspected SPI-pin damage from the
   reverse-battery/brownout events). New MAC 84:fc:e6:5e:67:58 — network
   RE-ONBOARDED 2026-07-13: DMZ reservation + NetBox + DNS re-pointed to new MAC,
   back on 192.168.15.60 / pipboy.empire12.net. ***
1x DS3231 RTC module + CR2032 coin cell (I2C 0x68) — clock without WiFi
1x MAX17048 LiPo fuel gauge board (I2C 0x36) — battery HP bar on STAT screen
   *** PHASED: not on hand yet. Firmware auto-detects it — build/run without
   it now (HP bar just stays dark), plug it in later, no reflash needed.
   Wire its cell-sense input to the PowerBoost BAT pad (raw cell). ***
1x LED driver on GPIO21 — AS BUILT: 2N3904 NPN for the 3-LED channel (base via
   1k + 10k pulldown). Upstream design uses a logic-level N-MOSFET (AO3400/
   IRLZ44N) for its ~70-LED bank; switch to that if the LED count grows past ~8.
